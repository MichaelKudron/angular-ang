A little bit about our porduct

At Długie Bagiety, we ensure that only the finest wheat is used in our baguettes. Here’s a brief overview of the wheat harvesting process that brings top-quality grain to our bakery.This meticulous process ensures that only the best wheat makes it into every Długie Bagiety baguette, delivering exceptional taste and quality.

Once the dough has properly fermented, it is carefully shaped by hand. This step requires skill and experience, as each baguette must be uniform in shape and size. Our bakers take great pride in this hands-on process, ensuring that each baguette is unique yet consistently excellent. This manual shaping is a testament to our commitment to traditional baking techniques and to maintaining the high standards of quality that define our products.

The final step is baking the baguettes in high-temperature ovens with steam. This crucial stage is what gives our baguettes their distinctive crispy, golden-brown crust while keeping the interior soft and moist. The steam creates the perfect baking environment, and our bakers closely monitor the process to ensure each baguette is baked to perfection.
